# About Me

Hello, thank you for coming over. I am a PhD Student in the [Laboratory for Industry 4.0 Smart Manufacturing Systems](https://lisms.auckland.ac.nz) at the Faculty of Engineering, University of Auckland. I am supervised by [Professor Xun Xu](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/x-xu) and [Dr. Jan Polzer](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/jan-polzer). My research interests include **smart manufacturing systems**, **autonomous manufacturing**, and **industrial AI**. My current research focuses on agentic manufacturing, aiming to develop intelligent, autonomous manufacturing systems capable of self-organising manufacturing resources.

## 🎓 Education

* **Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering**, *University of Auckland, New Zealand* (2024 – Present)  
  [Laboratory for Industry 4.0 Smart Manufacturing Systems](https://lisms.auckland.ac.nz)

* **M.Eng. in software engineering**, *University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC)* (2021 – 2024)  
  Worked in the [Shensi Lab](http://www.ultraxlab.com) on intelligent roll forming and digital twin manufacturing.

* **B.Eng. in computer science**, *Zhengzhou University, China* (2017 – 2021)

## 🔥 Research Focus

1. **Agentic Manufacturing Systems** – Autonomous and intelligent manufacturing systems capable of self-organizing and coordinating manufacturing resources through multi-agent collaboration.
2. **Industrial AI** – Data and learning-driven methods for process optimization, adaptive control, and decision-making in manufacturing.
3. **Digital Twin and Smart Manufacturing** – Integration of digital twins and cyber-physical systems for connected, resilient production environments.

# 关于我

你好，感谢你的访问。我是新西兰奥克兰大学工程学院[工业4.0智能制造系统实验室](https://lisms.auckland.ac.nz)的博士研究生，导师为 [Xun Xu 教授](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/x-xu) 和 [Jan Polzer 博士](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/jan-polzer)。我的研究方向包括**智能制造系统**、**自主制造**和**工业人工智能**。当前研究聚焦于智能体制造（Agentic Manufacturing），旨在开发能够自组织制造资源的智能自主制造系统。

## 🎓 教育经历

* **机械工程博士**, *新西兰奥克兰大学* (2024 – 至今)  
  [工业4.0智能制造系统实验室](https://lisms.auckland.ac.nz)

* **软件工程硕士**, *电子科技大学 (UESTC)* (2021 – 2024)  
  在[深思实验室](http://www.ultraxlab.com)从事智能辊压成形与数字孪生制造研究。

* **计算机科学与技术学士**, *郑州大学* (2017 – 2021)

## 🔥 研究方向

1. **智能体制造系统** – 通过多智能体协作，实现制造资源自组织与协调的自主智能制造系统。
2. **工业人工智能** – 面向制造过程优化、自适应控制与决策的数据与学习驱动方法。
3. **数字孪生与智能制造** – 数字孪生与信息物理系统的集成，构建互联、韧性的生产环境。
