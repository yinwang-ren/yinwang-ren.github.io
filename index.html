About Me

Hello, thank you for coming over. I am a PhD Student in the Laboratory for Industry 4.0 Smart Manufacturing Systems at the Faculty of Engineering, University of Auckland. I am supervised by Professor Xun Xu and Dr. Jan Polzer. My research interests include smart manufacturing systems, autonomous manufacturing, and industrial AI. My current research focuses on agentic manufacturing, aiming to develop intelligent, autonomous manufacturing systems capable of self-organising manufacturing resources.

关于我

你好，感谢你的访问。我是新西兰奥克兰大学工程学院工业4.0智能制造系统实验室的博士研究生，导师为 Xun Xu 教授和 Jan Polzer 博士。我的研究方向包括智能制造系统、自主制造和工业人工智能。当前研究聚焦于智能体制造（Agentic Manufacturing），旨在开发能够自组织制造资源的智能自主制造系统。

🔥 Research Focus

  1. Agentic Manufacturing Systems – Autonomous and intelligent manufacturing systems capable of self-organizing and coordinating manufacturing resources through multi-agent collaboration.
  2. Industrial AI – Data and learning-driven methods for process optimization, adaptive control, and decision-making in manufacturing.
  3. Digital Twin and Smart Manufacturing – Integration of digital twins and cyber-physical systems for connected, resilient production environments.

🔥 研究方向

  1. 智能体制造系统 – 通过多智能体协作，实现制造资源自组织与协调的自主智能制造系统。
  2. 工业人工智能 – 面向制造过程优化、自适应控制与决策的数据与学习驱动方法。
  3. 数字孪生与智能制造 – 数字孪生与信息物理系统的集成，构建互联、韧性的生产环境。
